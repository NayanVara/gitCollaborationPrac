AI in healthcare uses technology to improve efficiency and outcomes across administration, diagnosis, treatment, and research. Key applications include analyzing medical images like X-rays, discovering drugs, personalizing treatment plans, automating administrative tasks, and predicting patient risk. While promising, its successful implementation requires proper testing, data governance, regulatory frameworks, and collaboration to ensure safety, equity, and effectiveness.
